# What is Signals & Sorcery?

Signals & Sorcery is a live generative music platform designed for real-time composition and performance. Describe the sounds you want in natural language, and watch as MIDI sequences are generated, synthesizer patches are configured, and your musical ideas come to life - all in real-time. It sits at the intersection of live performance and procedural generation, enabling new creative workflows for musicians and performers.

# Who is this for?

  • Live Performers looking to incorporate generative elements into their sets
  • Music Producers who want to quickly sketch musical ideas using text descriptions
  • Electronic Musicians working with MIDI and synthesizers (currently Surge XT)
  • Experimental Artists exploring the boundaries of procedural music generation
  • Anyone who finds traditional MIDI programming tedious and wants a more intuitive approach

# How does it work?

# Core Technology

  • Electron Desktop App: Native macOS application with chat-based interface
  • Native Audio Engine: Built-in C++ audio engine that hosts VST/AU plugins and handles MIDI/audio processing natively
  • Surge XT Integration: Automatically loads and configures synth patches based on sound descriptions
  • Deterministic FX Rack: Fixed 6-slot system (Instrument, EQ, Distortion, Delay, Reverb, Compression)

# Key Features

  • Text-to-MIDI Generation: Describe sounds ("punchy techno kick", "ambient pad chords") and get MIDI
  • Automatic Synth Configuration: Selects and configures appropriate Surge XT patches based on your descriptions
  • Natural Language Control: Create tracks, manage FX, control transport, all through text
  • Zero-Friction Setup: Plugins and presets auto-install on first launch
  • FX Preset System: Bundled EQ, compression, reverb, delay, and distortion presets
  • Real-Time Performance: Designed for live use with low-latency audio processing

# Current Limitations

  • macOS Only: Tested on M-series & Intel chips (Windows/Linux not supported)
  • Surge XT Dependency: Currently only works with Surge XT synthesizer
  • MIDI-Focused: Primarily designed for MIDI/synth-based production and performance

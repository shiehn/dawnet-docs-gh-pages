Signals & Sorcery

Generative Audio Workstation

Mac (Silicon) open in new window Mac (Intel) open in new window

Contract-Based Composing

Define musical contracts (key, chords, BPM, bars), then generate MIDI and audio layers within them. You compose with the results and keep full creative control, not a one-shot finished song.

Extensible Plugin SDK

Built-in synth, sample, and audio generators all run on the Plugin SDK. Upcoming integrations include Splice, ElevenLabs, live coding, and agentic prompting.

Free Tier Available

No credit card required to get started. Explore generative composition, MIDI and audio generation, and the plugin ecosystem on the free tier before deciding to upgrade.

# Compose with infinite sounds

Define a musical contract, then generate endless MIDI and audio layers within it. MIDI via Gemini, audio via Stable Audio and Lyria. Signals & Sorcery is not one-shot song generation. It is a creative AI platform for building, previewing, shaping, and performing music with full control.
